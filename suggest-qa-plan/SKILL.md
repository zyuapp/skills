We just finished a new feature/product and we need to ensure its quality before release. Your task is to create a comprehensive QA plan that covers all aspects of testing for this new feature/product. Do not suggest unit/integration tests, as those are already being handled by the development process. Instead, focus on e2e local manual verification that could simulate real user interactions and scenarios. Go as deep as possible and cover all possible edge cases and scenarios that could arise in real-world usage.
